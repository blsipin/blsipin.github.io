Introduction

Vintage Fashion and Archives throughout the Decades

We have decided to focus on pulling old vintage fashion looks from previous runways and collections. We will analyze how trends have changed throughout the years and what we personally love. We want to focus on the simple, timeless looks that we are inspired by and see everyday.
